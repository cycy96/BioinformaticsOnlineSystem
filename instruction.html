How to proceed?

1) Choose the moltype that you want to analyze.
2) Enter the DNA or protein sequence you want to analyze into the box.
(Attention: Please make sure that all the letters are non-capital! Otherwise, information cannot be retrived completely.)
3) Press 'Submit' to perform the sequence analysis.
4) Press 'Reset' to clear all the fields.
5) Press 'Cancel' to exit the sequence analysis.
